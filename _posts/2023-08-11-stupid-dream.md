Vapor trail &  
stupid dream  
a sense of water  
primal scream

Empty vessel  
(give it up)  
the tips of your shoes  
next to each other

Gentle, brittle, gossamer touch  
a life in ever-bigger stumbles  
(drinking from a solid cup)  
accident prone  
disrupted & observed in  
vapid travels

Second skin atop  
the dreamers  
a hidden hand, a  
push and pull  
ripple reality

Dried-up tears amongst  
the sleepers  
washed away  
remember to forget  
leave the door ajar  
remember to regret  
there you were, but  
Here you are.
