See you in a  
microwave minute  
you happysad   
me super flanker  

our secret name  
be pigs, pigs, pigs  
kinetic kinship  
six-six-six  

It boils my blood  
emerged from a  
puddle of  
mud  

Underneath a  
Catherine Wheel  
I will  
return  

My arms extended  
a lesser man  
with gaze  
suspended  

And eyes that scan  
through fickle goods  
we only share this  
animal blood
