A gentle breeze  
My father's house  
Is full of feathers  

Zooming by thunder   
Shatter the seed  
Shut the all-seeing eye  

A wavering figure  
Firebird  
Crumbled letters  

The birth-flame ascends  
Yours are gnashing teeth  
But mine are wringing hands
