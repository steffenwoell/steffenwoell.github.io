I breathe your breath  
Friend of the night  
My cherished flower  

Blown  
Into collapsing  
Waves  

Endearing in daydreams  
Pastiche of endless  
Jaded hours  

Furniture all white  
What are you  
Trying to show  

Face  
or  
Mask  

Your sweet-sweet  
Name  
Kaputt
