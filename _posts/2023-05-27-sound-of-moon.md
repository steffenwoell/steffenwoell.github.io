Aquarian homestead  
Breathing through roots  
Arrested by  
A longer embrace  
Umbilical fingers  
Snip snap  
Moon burn  
Wash away  
Flip flop  
Pharynx, abysm  
Refracting the eyes  
Drip drop  
Sweeter water  
Flowing in  
This nightly maw  
A star to follow  
A moon too hollow  
Swallow
